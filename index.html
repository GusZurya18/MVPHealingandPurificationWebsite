Past Life Regression Therapy

Past Life Regression Therapy (PLRT) uses hypnosis to explore memories believed to come from previous lives, with the aim of finding insights and healing for problems experienced in current life.

Foundation and Purpose

The basis is the belief in reincarnation, that the human soul will experience many lives with different bodies and circumstances to grow and develop. The purpose is to overcome emotional, mental, or physical problems believed to originate from unresolved trauma or issues in past lives, and to gain deeper understanding of life purpose and recurring life patterns.

Therapy Process

  • Preparation: The therapist will explain the therapy process, discuss the client's goals and expectations, and create a comfortable and safe atmosphere
  • Hypnosis: The therapist guides the client into a deep relaxation state through hypnosis techniques, such as breathing guidance and visualization
  • Past Life Exploration: After entering a hypnotic state, the therapist will ask questions or provide guidance to help the client access past life memories
  • Processing and Healing: After accessing past life memories, the therapist works with the client to explore and process emerging emotions and experiences
  • Emerging from Hypnosis: After the exploration and processing session is complete, the therapist guides the client out of the hypnotic state slowly and safely

Sacred Purification at Pura Telaga Waja

Natural Spring Water Purification Site located within the sacred area of Pura Telaga Waja, Banjar Kepitu, Desa Kendran, Kecamatan Tegallalang, Kabupaten Gianyar – Bali. This sacred and culturally meaningful place is not merely a place for physical cleansing, but also a spiritual space that has been an inseparable part of local community life since ancient times.

Ancient Heritage

This purification site was not built arbitrarily, but is an ancestral heritage from the Kingdom of Puri Agung Kendran that has existed for centuries. The ancestors, with wisdom and deep knowledge of nature and spirituality, built this purification site by utilizing natural spring water sources flowing directly from the earth in an area considered sacred by the local community.

The 11 Sacred Spouts

What makes this purification site so special is the presence of 11 water spouts flowing forcefully and clearly, as well as a purification pool formed from natural stone. Each spout has its own meaning and function in the purification process, where each stage of cleansing at different spouts is believed to bring specific spiritual benefits.

Important Rules

To maintain the sanctity and harmony of this sacred place, there are important rules that must be seriously adhered to:

  • No clothing or fabric of any kind is permitted during the purification process
  • Men and women must take turns according to the order determined and in accordance with temple regulations

We hope everyone who comes here can feel the peace and sanctity given by this sacred place, and can appreciate and preserve the cultural heritage given by our ancestors to future generations.
